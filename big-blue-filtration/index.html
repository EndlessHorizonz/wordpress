big blue filtration

The BIG BLUE filtration is an all-in-one system, designed to make it easy to filter both harvested and municipal back-up water, that are stored in tanks containing chlorine floaters ,to a point where it is safe to drink. BIG BLUE filtration systems are available in various sizes so you can choose the system that suits your needs and your budget. The 3 stage system delivers water from your storage tanks to your house via an extremely efficient system. Not only is the water clean enough to drink it is also suited to a variety of domestic uses, from showering or bathing to running household appliances .You can also use the water to irrigate the garden and wash your car. with BIG BLUE filtration systems, you won't have to worry about supplying your family with clean and safe water. Cheers to that!
